Anshika ji… 🤍

Mujhe pata hai aap kyun gussa ho… Pehle main aapko bina bole samajh jata tha… aur shayad ab main utna dhyaan nahi de pa raha.

Aap meri aadat ho… aur aadatein chhodi nahi jaati, sambhali jaati hain. 💖

Can we fix this… together?
